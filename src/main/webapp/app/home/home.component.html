QualOpt is a platform for researchers to manage the survey invitations for their studies, and find participants to send them to via GitHub. It also provides the ability for GitHub users to opt out of receiving survey invitations. The goal of this is to address the issue of GitHub users receiving too many unsolicited survey invitations

For Researchers

Researchers can use this application to send out survey invitations in moderation. You can create a study on the application, with details of your survey and draft the email to be sent to your participants. Additionally, you can filter the participants who will receive the survey invitations using information from GitHub

For Study Participants

Participants may choose to opt out of receiving survey invitations
